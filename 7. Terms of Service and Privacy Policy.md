### 1. Introduction

KiraKita ("we", "our", "the Application") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA Malaysia)**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use KiraKita.

By using KiraKita you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described herein.

---

### 2. Information We Collect

**2.1 Information from Google OAuth**

When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Google Account ID
- Profile picture URL

We do not collect your Google password. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's secure OAuth 2.0 system.

**2.2 Information You Provide**

When using the Application you may provide:
- Session names and descriptions
- Expense descriptions and amounts
- Payment proof screenshots
- Reference documents (receipts, invoices, bills)
- Saved payment QR code (optional)
- Display name (guest users only)

**2.3 Information We Do Not Collect**

KiraKita does NOT collect:
- Bank account numbers directly (only visible in user-uploaded screenshots)
- Credit or debit card information
- National identification numbers
- Location data
- Device information beyond standard browser data

---

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| User authentication | Google ID, email, name | Consent via Google OAuth |
| Session management | Name, email | Contractual necessity |
| Expense tracking | Expense entries | User-provided consent |
| Payment verification | Payment proof screenshots | User-provided consent |
| Profile display | Name, avatar | Legitimate interest |
| Session history | Session records | User-provided consent |

We do NOT use your data for:
- Marketing or advertising purposes
- Sale to third parties
- Profiling or automated decision making
- Any purpose beyond operating the Application

---

### 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**4.1 Within Sessions**
Payment proofs, reference documents, and expense records are visible to all participants within the same session. By joining a session you consent to other session participants viewing your uploaded content.

**4.2 Third Party Services**
KiraKita uses the following third party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Google OAuth 2.0 | Authentication | Handled by Google — we receive profile data only |
| Cloudinary | File storage | Uploaded images stored on Cloudinary servers |

**4.3 Cloudinary and Cross-Border Data Transfer**
Please be aware that uploaded files including payment proofs and reference documents are stored on Cloudinary servers which are located outside Malaysia. By uploading files to KiraKita you consent to this cross-border transfer of your data. For a future commercial deployment KiraKita intends to migrate to Malaysia-approved storage providers in full compliance with Section 129 of the PDPA 2010.

**4.4 Legal Disclosure**
We may disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to valid legal processes from Malaysian authorities.

**4.5 No Sale of Data**
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

---

### 5. Data Storage and Security

**5.1 Storage Location**
- User account data is stored in a MySQL database on our hosting server
- Uploaded files are stored on Cloudinary with private authenticated access
- No uploaded file is publicly accessible via direct URL

**5.2 Access Controls**
- All uploaded files are stored as private assets accessible only through authenticated application requests
- Session data is scoped strictly to session participants — no one outside the session can access your data
- Role-based access control ensures each user can only access data relevant to their role

**5.3 Security Measures**
We implement the following security measures to protect your data:
- Google OAuth 2.0 — no passwords stored locally
- UUID-based session URLs — prevents unauthorized session access
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Private Cloudinary URLs — uploaded files not publicly accessible
- Session-scoped data visibility — participants only see their own session data
- Authenticated file serving — all file access requires valid session membership

**5.4 Data Breach**
In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data we will notify affected users as soon as reasonably practicable in accordance with PDPA requirements.

---

### 6. Data Retention

**6.1 Session Files**
All uploaded files associated with a session including payment proofs and reference documents are permanently deleted from Cloudinary when the host closes the session.

**6.2 Session Records**
Expense records and session history are retained in registered user accounts until the account is deleted.

**6.3 Guest Data**
Guest users' data including display names and uploaded files are permanently deleted upon session closure. Guests do not have persistent data storage.

**6.4 Account Data**
Registered user account data including name, email, and profile information is retained until account deletion is requested.

**6.5 Saved Payment QR**
If you choose to save your payment QR to your profile it is retained until you explicitly delete it from your profile settings or request account deletion.

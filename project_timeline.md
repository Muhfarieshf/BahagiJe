The project timeline is organized into eight distinct operational phases, which are visually mapped and presented in the Gantt chart designated as Figure 4. The timeline initiates with the Proposal phase in Week 1, spanning from May 18 to May 24, 2026, which is dedicated entirely to the finalization and submission of the formal project proposal. This transitions directly into the System Design phase in Week 2, running from May 25 to May 31, 2026, which focuses on completing the entity relationship diagram, database schema, user interface wireframes, and the three-tier client-server architectural configuration.

System development is subsequently executed through five sequential agile sprints. Sprint 1 in Week 3, from June 1 to June 7, 2026, establishes the core application foundations, including Google OAuth authentication scaffolding, database migrations, and user login modules. Sprint 2 in Week 4, from June 8 to June 14, 2026, introduces privacy-focused QR code generation with UUID integration, the session creation form with its dynamic preset engine, and the administrative host control panel. This leads into Sprint 3 in Week 5, spanning June 15 to June 21, 2026, which focuses on the core calculation logic by developing the participant item logging module, the proportional split formula, and the net-debt settlement algorithm. Next, Sprint 4 in Week 6, running from June 22 to June 28, 2026, implements the payment verification layer, including the payment proof upload module, host approval and rejection workflows, a live payment status dashboard, and session-scoped receipt privacy boundaries. Sprints conclude with Sprint 5 in Week 7, from June 29 to July 7, 2026, which covers user interface polishing, data encryption implementation, cross-device functional and usability testing, and prototype validation.

The final operational stretch prioritizes documentation, submission, and defense. The Technical Report phase in Week 8, from July 8 to July 14, 2026, encompasses comprehensive writing, technical proofreading, and formatting of the final proposal manuscript. Lastly, the Presentation phase in Week 9, from July 19 to July 31, 2026, covers presentation slide preparation, live deployment dry runs, and the final presentation milestone.
